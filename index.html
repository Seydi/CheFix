Just what the HELL is CheFix?

Are you hungry, broke, and lazy? If that's the case, CheFix is the App for you. Just a few taps and you will be eating a high quality homecooked meal in a matter of minutes.

Here are a few of the many features of CheFix:

A $5 only price per meal. That's right! For as little as five bucks, you can eat the homecooked meal of your choice.

A state of the art mapping system that enables you to quickly connect to cooks within your immediate vacinity.

A five star rating system for each cook you order from to ensure the quality of your meals and service.

Search fiters to make sure you eat ON YOUR TERMS. Distance, cuisine, ratings, cooking time, quantities, etc.

Many more amazing features are still in the works. Our goal is to make CheFix as deliciously awesome as possible.
